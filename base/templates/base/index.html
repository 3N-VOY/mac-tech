Features

The Mac-Tech Pro II Series features next-level imaging performance. Mac-Tech Pro II's triple-camera system ushers in a new era of camera drones by housing three sensors and lenses with different focal lengths. Equipped with a Hasselblad camera and dual tele cameras, Mac-Tech II Pro is a triple-camera drone that unlocks new shooting perspectives, allowing you to embrace creative freedom further, capture fascinating scenery, explore photographic storytelling, and make cinematic masterpieces.

The Mac-Tech Pro II is designed to be incredibly compact and highly portable, fitting comfortably in the palm of your hand. This remarkable level of portability means you can take your drone with you on all your adventures, whether you're exploring new landscapes or capturing memories at family gatherings.

Regardless of your experience level, flying the Mac-Tech Pro II is a breeze. Its user-friendly controls provide a smooth and stable flight experience. Even if you're a first-time pilot, you'll quickly feel in command of your drone. For seasoned pros, the Mac-Tech Pro II offers precise control and responsiveness that will impress.

With a built-in high-definition camera, the Mac-Tech Pro II elevates your photography game to new heights. Capture breathtaking photos and videos from the skies with precision and clarity. Share your unique perspective and unlock the world of aerial photography.

The Mac-Tech Pro II's efficient battery ensures you can enjoy extended flight time. Spend less time worrying about recharging and more time exploring your surroundings and capturing every moment from above. It's your ticket to endless adventures.

he Mac-Tech Pro II comes equipped with a range of intelligent features. The auto-hover function keeps your drone in a stable position, headless mode simplifies orientation, and one-button takeoff and landing make flying hassle-free. These smart features ensure that your drone is accessible to pilots of all skill levels.
